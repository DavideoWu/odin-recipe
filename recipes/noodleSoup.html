Tom Yum Noodle Soup

Tom Yum Noodle Soup is a take on the famous spicy soup from Thailand

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Fishballs
  • Rice noodles
  • Tom yum noodle paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil rice noodles in a pot.
  2. Drain rice noodles in collander.
  3. Put olive oil in pot, stir fry shrimp for a bit.
  4. Add water to pot, wait until boiling.
  5. Put in some Tom Yum Paste
  6. Put in some fish sauce
  7. Put in fishballs, veggies, anything else.
  8. Cook a bit more, serve.
