Egg Croissant Breakfast Sandwich

A simple breakfast sandwich for a quick morning energizer.

Ingredients

  • Croissant
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 sausage
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Cook 2 fried eggs
  2. Fry 1 sausage
  3. Toast 2 croissants
  4. Place 2 eggs and sausage between croissants. Add cheese
  5. Serve
